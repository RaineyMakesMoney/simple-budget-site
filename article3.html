Managing Debt While Budgeting

Home | How to Budget | Tools & Templates | Tips & Articles

Paying off debt while sticking to a budget can feel overwhelming. Here are some tips to manage both effectively:

  1. List all debts: Include balances, interest rates, and minimum payments.
  2. Prioritize high-interest debt: Pay more on the debt with the highest interest while making minimum payments on others.
  3. Include debt payments in your budget: Treat them like any other fixed expense.
  4. Look for extra income: Temporary side jobs or selling unused items can help accelerate repayment.
  5. Track your progress: Use our budget calculator to see how much faster you can pay off debt with extra payments.
