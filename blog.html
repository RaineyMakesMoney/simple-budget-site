Budgeting Tips

  • Track your spending: Keep a daily log of expenses to understand where your money goes.
  • Create realistic categories: Use categories you actually spend money on, not just theoretical ones.
  • Pay yourself first: Save a portion of income before spending on anything else.
  • Set financial goals: Short-term and long-term goals make budgeting more meaningful.
  • Review monthly: Check your budget every month and adjust as needed.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not budgeting for irregular expenses (like car repairs or gifts).
  • Overestimating income or underestimating expenses.
  • Ignoring small daily purchases — they add up quickly.
  • Failing to revisit your budget — budgets are flexible, not static.
