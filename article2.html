How to Build an Emergency Fund

Home | How to Budget | Tools & Templates | Tips & Articles

An emergency fund can protect you from unexpected expenses like car repairs or medical bills. Follow these steps to build one:

  1. Set a goal: Start with $500-$1,000, then gradually build up to 3-6 months of expenses.
  2. Open a separate account: Keep your emergency savings separate from your checking account.
  3. Automate savings: Set up a recurring transfer from your paycheck to your emergency fund.
  4. Cut unnecessary expenses: Use our budget tools to find areas where you can save.
  5. Start small and stay consistent: Even $10 a week adds up over time.
