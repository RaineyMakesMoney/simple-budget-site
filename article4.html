Budgeting as a Couple

Home | How to Budget | Tools & Templates | Tips & Articles

Budgeting as a couple requires communication and planning. Here are some strategies to make it work:

  1. Set shared goals: Agree on short-term and long-term financial goals together.
  2. Decide on budgeting method: Combine finances, split bills, or use a hybrid approach.
  3. Track expenses together: Use a shared spreadsheet or our budget calculator.
  4. Regularly review finances: Set a monthly check-in to discuss spending, savings, and upcoming expenses.
  5. Respect individual allowances: Give each person a small “fun money” budget to avoid resentment.
